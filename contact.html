Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website's order tracking page or directly on the courier's website to check your delivery status.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 14 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return process.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates vary depending on location and package weight. At checkout, you'll see available shipping options and costs for your location.
How can I change or cancel my order?
To change or cancel an order, please contact us immediately at e.samosino2017@gmail.com or call 0910 354 0326. We can usually accommodate changes if the order hasn't been processed yet. Once an order is shipped, it cannot be canceled.
